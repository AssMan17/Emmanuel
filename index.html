About Me

Web Designer & Aspiring Artist

Hello there my name is Emmanuel, a front-end web designer and an aspiring artist from Philippines who created Artisan Express. My work is inspired by famous painter and mangaka artist such as Takehiko Inoue, Kentaro Muira and John Singer Sargent. Through using traditional methods, I am focused on creating bold work showcasing my obsessions with Japanese traditions, my region and heritage, and 19th century paintings.

Art washes away from the soul the dust of everyday life.
